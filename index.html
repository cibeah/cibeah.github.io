About

I am a PhD candidate in the Inria Paris and IFP Energies Nouvelles common research lab. Within Inria Paris, I am part of the Argo team, affiliated with the DI ENS, where I am advised by Prof. Ana Bušić. At IFP Energies Nouvelles, I was lucky to be advised by Jiamin Zhu and Donatien Dubuc. My PhD work is on multi-agent reinforcement learning algorithms for the dynamic wind farm control problem. More broadly, I want to understand how learning agents can cooperate, and how can this can help us solve real problems.
