About

I am currently a postdoctoral researcher in the PERSEE center of Mines Paris PSL, working in the ERSEI team led by Georges Kariniotakis. Before that, I did a PhD at Inria Paris (Argo team in DI ENS ) and IFP Energies Nouvelles, working on multi-agent reinforcement learning algorithms for the dynamic wind farm control problem. I was advised by Prof. Ana Bušić at Inria Paris, and Jiamin Zhu and Donatien Dubuc at IFP Energies Nouvelles. More broadly, I want to understand how learning agents can cooperate to solve real-world problems.
